Infrastructure

Four services, one platform

Convert clinical documents, process insurance claims, anonymize clinical data/images, and detect forgery. Built and runs on unified infrastructure.

Convert

Clinical Document

Transform raw clinical PDFs (discharge summaries & diagnostic reports) into ABDM-compliant FHIR R4 JSON bundles.

  • FHIR R4 validation
  • Scanned & printed record support
  • LangGraph orchestrated extraction
Verify

Forgery Detection

Classical forensic analysis to detect digital manipulation, splicing, and tampering in clinical documents and claim forms.

  • Error Level Analysis (ELA)
  • PDF & image support
  • Real-time processing
Process

Insurance Policy

Process insurance policies into validated NHCX JSON formats with dependency-aware topological extraction.

  • NHCX schema validation
  • Sub-limit & waiting period extraction
  • Complex policy term parsing
Protect

Privacy Filter (Anonymization)

Anonymize medical images, scans, and clinical documents by removing burned-in patient text, metadata, and PII on CPU with zero data retention.

  • DPDP Act compliant
  • DICOM, NIfTI, image & PDF support
  • Interactive review
