About TANUH

TANUH (Translational AI for Networked Universal Healthcare) is a Section 8 not-for-profit promoted by the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. Funded by the Ministry of Education as an AI Centre of Excellence in Healthcare, TANUH builds secure, AI-driven Digital Public Infrastructure for India's healthcare ecosystem.
