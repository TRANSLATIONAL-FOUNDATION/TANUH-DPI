About Us

TANUH (Translational Artificial Intelligence for Networked Universal Healthcare) is a Section 8 not-for-profit organization promoted by Indian Institute of Science (IISc). Funded by the Ministry of Education as an AI Centre of Excellence in Healthcare, TANUH is dedicated to advancing the Government of India's vision to "Make AI in India and Make AI Work for India."

TANUH focuses on building secure, AI-driven, scalable Digital Public Infrastructure that empowers healthcare providers, insurers, and patients. Its current suite of tools supports Clinical Document Evaluation and Insurance Policy Processing, while also incorporating automated Privacy Filters for data redaction and rigorous Forgery Detection mechanisms. Through these innovations, TANUH delivers an end-to-end ecosystem built on efficiency, security, and trust. Ultimately, TANUH’s AI technology is purpose-built to provide intelligent, secure, and transformative healthcare solutions for India.
